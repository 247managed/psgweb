Our Approach

A Safe Space for Healing and Growth

We believe that seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Our practice is built on the understanding that every person's journey is unique and deserves individualized attention and care.

At Paula S. Gordy LISW, LLC, we use evidence-based therapeutic approaches in a warm, nonjudgmental environment. Whether you are dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, trauma, grief, or life transitions, our clinicians will work collaboratively with you to identify your goals and develop a personalized treatment plan.

We are committed to serving the communities of southern Iowa, offering convenient locations and flexible scheduling to make accessing care as easy as possible. Your mental health matters, and we are honored to be a part of your journey toward wellness.
