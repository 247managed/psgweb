About Us

Compassionate mental health care for southern Iowa, built on trust, empathy, and evidence-based practice.

Our Story

Dedicated to Your Mental Health & Well-Being

Paula S. Gordy LISW, LLC is a dedicated outpatient mental health practice proudly serving the communities of southern Iowa. Founded with a clear mission to provide accessible, compassionate, and quality mental health services, our practice supports individuals and families on their path to healing and growth.

As a Licensed Independent Social Worker (LISW) practice, we bring professional expertise, genuine care, and a deep understanding of the unique challenges facing our communities. With three convenient locations in Centerville, Ottumwa, and Bloomfield, we are committed to meeting you where you are and making mental health care accessible to all.

Our team is grounded in evidence-based, trauma-informed care. We believe that every person deserves a safe, supportive space where they can explore their thoughts and feelings without judgment. Whether you are navigating anxiety, depression, grief, family conflict, or any of life's challenges, we are here to walk alongside you.

  • Licensed Independent Social Worker (LISW) practice
  • Serving Centerville, Ottumwa, Bloomfield, and surrounding areas
  • Evidence-based, trauma-informed care
  • Dedicated to accessible mental health services for all

What Guides Us

Our Mission & Values

To provide compassionate, accessible, and effective mental health services that empower individuals and families to lead healthier, more fulfilling lives.

Compassion

We meet every client with empathy and understanding. Your feelings are valid, and our team is here to listen without judgment, offering genuine support at every step of your journey.

Accessibility

With three locations serving southern Iowa and acceptance of most major insurance plans and Iowa Medicaid, we work to remove barriers and make quality mental health care available to everyone who needs it.

Evidence-Based Care

We use proven therapeutic approaches grounded in current research and best practices. Our commitment to ongoing professional development ensures you receive the most effective, up-to-date treatment available.

Client-Centered

Your goals guide our work together. We honor your individual experiences and collaborate with you to develop a personalized treatment plan that reflects your unique needs, strengths, and aspirations.

Integrity & Confidentiality

We uphold the highest standards of ethical practice and are fully HIPAA-compliant. Your privacy and trust are sacred to us, and we are committed to maintaining a safe, confidential therapeutic environment.

How We Help

Our Approach to Care

At Paula S. Gordy LISW, LLC, we believe that meaningful change begins within a safe, supportive therapeutic relationship. Our approach is built on a foundation of respect, collaboration, and proven methods that have helped countless individuals and families find their way forward.

  • Client-Centered Care: We place you at the center of everything we do, tailoring each session to your unique needs and goals.
  • Trauma-Informed Approach: We understand the impact of trauma and create an environment that promotes safety, trust, and healing.
  • Evidence-Based Modalities: Our therapists utilize proven therapeutic methods backed by current clinical research and best practices.
  • Culturally Sensitive Practice: We honor and respect the diverse backgrounds, experiences, and identities of every client we serve.
  • Collaborative Relationship: Therapy is a partnership. We work together with you, honoring your perspective and fostering a strong therapeutic alliance.
  • Empowerment & Skill-Building: We focus on helping you develop the tools and skills you need to navigate challenges and thrive beyond the therapy room.
